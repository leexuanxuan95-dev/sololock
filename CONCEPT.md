## Core Mechanics
1. Pick session: 15min / 1h / 4h / overnight
2. Pick lockmaster:
   - **AI judge** — no early unlock, period
   - **Random delay** — early unlock requires waiting 15 min + writing a 50-word reason
   - **Paid commitment** — $5 charged if you break early (donated to charity, your choice)
   - **Friend** — optional invite (legacy LOCKBOX mode)
3. Lock activates → Screen Time blocks all selected apps (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, etc.)
4. Live Activity countdown on Lock Screen
5. Emergency unlock available always (long press 5s, but logged)
6. Session complete → small celebration + history log
